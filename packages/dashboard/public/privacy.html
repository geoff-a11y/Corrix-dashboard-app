Privacy Policy

Corrix Chrome Extension

Last Updated: December 29, 2024

Human Machines Group LLC ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Corrix Chrome extension ("Extension"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Extension.

Summary

Corrix is designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • All analysis runs locally in your browser
  • Conversation content is never transmitted to our servers
  • ML models run on-device via Transformers.js
  • You control what data, if any, is shared

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information Processed Locally (Never Transmitted)

The following information is processed entirely within your browser and is never sent to our servers:

  • Your prompts and AI responses
  • Conversation content
  • Text you type or copy
  • The specific content of your interactions with AI platforms

1.2 Information Stored Locally

The following is stored in your browser's local storage:

  • Your collaboration scores and metrics
  • Notification preferences and settings
  • Session statistics (prompt counts, response times)
  • Coaching tip effectiveness data
  • Skill progression data

1.3 Information Collected with Your Consent

If you choose to create an account and enable cloud sync, we collect:

  • Account Information: Email address (via Clerk authentication)
  • Anonymized Usage Metrics: Aggregated, non-identifiable patterns such as:
    • Average prompt quality scores
    • Verification behavior rates
    • Session duration statistics
    • Notification response patterns
  • Organization Data (Enterprise users only): Team membership, aggregated team scores

We do not collect:

  • The content of your prompts or AI responses
  • Personal identifying information beyond email
  • Browsing history outside of supported AI platforms
  • Keyboard or mouse activity

2. How We Use Information

2.1 Local Processing

We use locally-processed information to:

  • Analyze your AI collaboration patterns
  • Generate personalized coaching nudges
  • Calculate your Collaboration Results Index scores
  • Provide session insights and weekly summaries

2.2 Cloud-Synced Data (Optional)

If you enable cloud sync, we use your anonymized data to:

  • Sync your scores across devices
  • Provide dashboard access to your historical data
  • Generate organizational insights (Enterprise only)
  • Improve our coaching algorithms through aggregate analysis

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Authentication (Clerk)

We use Clerk for secure authentication. When you sign in:

  • Clerk processes your email and authentication credentials
  • We receive only your email and a user identifier
  • See Clerk's privacy policy: https://clerk.com/privacy

3.2 Data Storage (Supabase)

For users with cloud sync enabled:

  • Anonymized metrics are stored in Supabase
  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • See Supabase's privacy policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

3.3 AI Platforms

The Extension operates on Claude.ai (Anthropic), ChatGPT (OpenAI), and Gemini (Google). We do not send any data to these platforms. We only observe your interactions within your browser to provide coaching.

3.4 Machine Learning Models

We use Hugging Face Transformers.js to run ML models:

  • All models run entirely in your browser
  • No data is sent to Hugging Face servers
  • Models are cached locally after first download

4. Data Retention

4.1 Local Data

  • Session data: Cleared when you close your browser or manually clear
  • Preferences: Retained until you uninstall the Extension
  • Historical scores: Retained locally for up to 90 days

4.2 Cloud Data (If Enabled)

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active
  • Usage metrics: Retained for up to 2 years for trend analysis
  • Deleted upon account deletion request

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share anonymized, aggregated data:

  • With enterprise customers (their organization's aggregate data only)
  • For research purposes (fully anonymized, no individual identification possible)
  • As required by law

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Export

You can:

  • View all locally stored data via the Extension's options page
  • Export your data in JSON format
  • Access cloud data through your dashboard

6.2 Deletion

You can:

  • Clear local data: Options → Clear All Data
  • Delete cloud account: Settings → Delete Account
  • Uninstall the Extension to remove all local data

6.3 Opt-Out

You can:

  • Use the Extension without creating an account (local-only mode)
  • Disable cloud sync at any time
  • Disable specific notification types
  • Adjust privacy settings in Options

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • HTTPS encryption for all network communications
  • Secure authentication via Clerk
  • No storage of sensitive content (prompts, responses)
  • Regular security reviews

8. Children's Privacy

The Extension is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via Extension update notes, email (if you have an account), or notice in the Extension.
